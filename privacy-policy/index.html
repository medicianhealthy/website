Wise Medical Apps ("we", "us", or "our") operates the Medication Wizard mobile application. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our App.

1. Privacy-First Architecture

We believe your health data is your own. The App is designed with a "local-first" approach. This means the vast majority of your data stays on your phone and is never transmitted to us.

2. Data Collection & Health Information

We do not collect personal health information on our servers. Medication names, dosages, and schedules are stored locally on your device. We do not have access to this data, nor can we sell or share it with third parties.

3. Third-Party Services

The App uses third-party services that may collect information used to identify you:

  • Google Play Services: For core Android functionality.
  • AdMob: To show advertisements.
  • Firebase Analytics: To help us understand how the app is used (anonymous data).
  • Google Drive: Only if you explicitly enable Cloud Backup.

4. Cloud Backup

If you choose to use the Cloud Backup feature, your data is synced to your own Google Drive account. We do not host this data on our own servers.

5. Security

The security of your data is important to us. We use standard mobile security practices to protect your locally stored information, but please remember that no method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your data. Since all data is stored locally, you can do this at any time by editing your medications or using the "Clear All Data" feature in settings.
